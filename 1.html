Overview

Purpose

Are you a BYU-I student who lives far from home? I know I am. I have this problem where I have more stuff than I can fit in a suitcase and a backpack, so I have to either find someone who can keep my extra stuff or throw it away. That’s what this company, DirectSHED is for! It’s a storage company where you can rent out a storage shed and leave your extra stuff there for when you come back the next semester. You can go to their website if you want more information on things like size, security, prices, and location.

Audience

DirectSHED is meant for BYU-I students who need more space to store their stuff in their off-semesters while they're at home. People who don't have cars to drive all of their stuff home with and don't want to throw it all away either are the type of people DirectSHED is for.

Typography

Heading Font: Arial Black

I chose Arial Black as my heading font because of its bold characteristic. I want it to be clear that these are headers, while at the same time maintaining a simple and easy to read font.

Paragraph Font: Segoe UI

I chose this font because it's a fairly simple font and doesn't stand out too much. Since there will be a lot of text in this font, I wanted it to be simple and very easy to read in paragraphs, and I think this font accomplishes that.

Home page

What is DirectSHED?

DirectSHED is a storage unit in Rexburg, Idaho built specifically for college students (though anybody is welcome to rent a unit) who live far from home and can’t bring all of their stuff home in between semesters. DISCLAIMER: DirectSHED is not a real business and none of the locations, numbers, prices, images, etc. are accurate depictions of these non-existent storage units.

Why DirectSHED?

DirectSHED is a high quality and cheaper alternative to the other storage units in Rexburg. Our storage units are more convenient than other units around town because of our close proximity to the BYU-I campus, our cheap, easy rentals, and our competitive price match system to other units close by. Our storage units also include a 24/7 security camera system for each individual unit to ensure your stuff’s safety!

Our philosophy

Here at DirectSHED, we believe in giving our customers the highest quality and cheapest option for a storage unit as possible! We understand the college struggle, and we want to help in the best way we know how: good units for low prices. Don’t miss out, we sell out quick at the start of each BYUI semester!

Prices

Our prices

For our storage units, we offer the first month with us for free! After that first free month, you can rent our units for $30.00 a month. Each storage unit is 10 feet by 8 feet large, along with a 10-foot ceiling height. We also offer a unique price matching system, unlike any other storage units in town. If you manage to find a cheaper unit in Rexburg, we will offer you a cheaper price than what that unit offers! We want our customers to be as confident as we are that their money is being well spent with us.

Office hours

We are so confident with our security systems set in place that you are actually able to access your rented unit anytime, even when we aren’t there! While you are able to access your unit during all 24 hours of the day, we only offer customer service during the daytime from 9am-5pm from Monday through Saturday.

Location

Our location

The DirectSHED storage units are located on 64 N 2nd E, Rexburg, ID 83440. We wanted to get our units as close as possible to the BYU-I campus (less than half a mile away!) to ensure that moving in and out of your apartments runs as smoothly as possible. Each storage unit is 10x8x10 feet. If you decide to give us a visit and are interested in renting a unit, you can talk to us at the front desk about more specific details and we can help you get it figured out!

Security

The DirectSHED facility has state of the arc technology to ensure your unit is as safe as possible to only you. We offer 24/7 security recording, along with unique two-factor authentication locks on each unit that can be connected to your phone (also includes physical key).
